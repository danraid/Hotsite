# Hotsite Section Framework

Choose sections based on visitor questions and conversion needs. Do not include every section by default.

## Required evaluation sequence

1. Header and navigation
2. Hero
3. Immediate trust or credibility
4. Problem/context recognition
5. Value proposition
6. How the professional can help
7. Approach or methodology
8. About the professional
9. Service modalities
10. Transition or emotional pause
11. How it works
12. FAQ
13. Final CTA
14. Footer and legal information

## Section contract

For every retained section define:

- Section ID
- Purpose
- Visitor question answered
- Required message
- Source content
- Evidence or verification need
- Primary CTA
- Secondary CTA
- Desktop behavior
- Mobile behavior
- Accessibility notes
- Analytics event
- Content owner

## Selection rules

- Merge sections with duplicate persuasion roles.
- Split sections only when content density or interaction requires it.
- Do not add testimonials without approval.
- Do not add pricing when pricing policy is unknown.
- Do not add FAQ answers that rely on invented service rules.
- Preserve the calm narrative rhythm and avoid repetitive CTAs.
