# Audience and Offer

## Current audience hypothesis

Adults who:

- feel emotionally overloaded, anxious, sad, disconnected, or unable to slow down;
- repeat patterns in relationships or decisions;
- experience excessive self-criticism or difficulty setting boundaries;
- are going through grief, separation, transition, or important choices;
- seek self-knowledge, emotional maturity, purpose, or meaning;
- prefer an individualized, reflective, discreet, and integrative approach.

This is a working hypothesis derived from supplied copy. Demographic, geographic, behavioral, and channel data remain `[OPEN]`.

## Likely awareness level

The homepage appears aimed at visitors who are:

- problem-aware;
- considering support;
- comparing approaches or professionals;
- not necessarily ready to book immediately.

The Design Doc should support a trust-building journey before asking for conversion.

## Core visitor questions

1. Is this space appropriate for what I am experiencing?
2. Will I be heard without judgment?
3. What type of work is offered?
4. Who is Janaína and what is her background?
5. How does the process begin?
6. Is care available online or in person?
7. How private and confidential is the process?
8. What should I do when I need medical or regulated health care?

## Primary offer

A private initial conversation to understand the visitor's current moment, explain how support works, and assess the most appropriate path.

## Service offers

- Individual sessions
- Family Constellation sessions
- Group experiences and workshops

## Desired transformation language

Use language around:

- understanding;
- awareness;
- emotional resources;
- clearer choices;
- healthier boundaries;
- meaning;
- integration;
- conscious relationships.

Do not promise cure, guaranteed relief, guaranteed prosperity, or universal outcomes.

## Primary objections to address

- Uncertainty about whether the service fits the visitor's needs
- Fear of judgment or exposure
- Uncertainty about the method
- Concern about confidentiality
- Concern about online versus in-person effectiveness or logistics
- Uncertainty about credentials and professional scope
- Uncertainty about price, duration, and next steps

## Offer details still required

- Session duration
- Session frequency
- Price or price-disclosure policy
- Cancellation/rescheduling policy
- Payment methods
- Geographic availability
- Age restrictions
- Online platform
- Physical location
- Intake process
- Emergency and crisis boundaries
