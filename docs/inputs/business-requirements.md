# Business Requirements

## Conversion requirements

- The homepage must expose `Agendar uma conversa` as the primary action.
- The homepage must expose service exploration as a secondary path.
- The final section must repeat the primary action and provide WhatsApp as a secondary contact channel.
- All conversion destinations must be configurable and not hard-coded across components.

## Content requirements

- Present Janaína's professional positioning, approach, trajectory, services, and process.
- Preserve the supplied calm, reflective tone.
- Avoid publishing unverified credentials, claims, or regulated titles.
- Provide clear integrated-care language where emotional or health conditions are mentioned.

## Experience requirements

- Mobile-first behavior is mandatory.
- The experience must be calm, readable, and low-friction.
- Navigation should support direct access to services, approach, about, how it works, and contact.
- Long content must be structured for scanning without flattening its emotional depth.
- Avoid intrusive popups, countdowns, autoplay media, and aggressive capture patterns.

## Trust requirements

- Credentials must be fact-checked.
- Contact identity and professional information must be easy to find.
- Privacy and consent must be explicit wherever personal data is collected.
- Avoid testimonials unless their use is legally, ethically, and professionally approved.
