# Legal, Professional, and Compliance Inputs

## Status

This document is a requirements checklist, not legal or professional advice. Final publication must be reviewed by the project owner and, where appropriate, qualified legal and professional advisors.

## Personal data

Potentially collected data may include:

- Name
- Telephone/WhatsApp
- Email
- Service interest
- Scheduling information
- Free-text message

The final data inventory depends on the approved contact flow.

## Required privacy decisions

- Data controller identity
- Legal basis and purpose for each field
- Retention period
- Access and deletion request channel
- Third-party processors
- International transfers, if any
- Cookie and analytics consent
- Secure storage and transmission

## Sensitive-data rule

Do not request detailed mental-health, diagnostic, medical, trauma, or crisis information through a general marketing form unless a qualified legal/security review explicitly approves the flow and safeguards.

## Professional and health-related content

Before publication, verify:

- Professional title and scope of practice
- Registration information, where applicable
- Accuracy of all credentials and institution names
- Permitted descriptions of methods and services
- Rules governing advertising and testimonials
- Required disclaimers
- Whether the word `terapêutico` and related terms are appropriate in the intended context

## Required safety boundary

The site must not present itself as an emergency or crisis service unless such a service genuinely exists and is staffed accordingly.

The contact flow should state what to do in urgent or emergency situations if the professional/legal review requires it.

## Claims rules

Do not publish:

- Guarantees of cure or outcome
- Claims that a modality treats a diagnosed condition without approved support and scope
- Claims that services replace medical, psychiatric, psychological, or other indicated care
- Unsupported superiority claims
- Claims that blame individuals for illness or lack of progress

## Existing integrated-care language

The supplied content states that care may be part of an integrated process and not a substitute for indicated professional assistance. Preserve this intent, subject to final review.

## Required pages or disclosures

- Privacy policy
- Cookie policy, when applicable
- Terms or service-use information, when applicable
- Professional identification/footer disclosure
- Contact information
- Consent text for forms
